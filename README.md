# Food-delivery-analytics-powerbi

# 🍔 Swiggy Sales & Delivery Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI.
The dashboard analyzes sales performance, customer activity, restaurant participation, and delivery efficiency across multiple cities.

The project is inspired by the food delivery platform **Swiggy** and aims to demonstrate how data visualization can help understand order trends, city performance, and operational efficiency in a food delivery business.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze total revenue generated from food orders
* Monitor customer and restaurant activity
* Compare sales performance across different cities
* Track average delivery time by city
* Understand demand for Veg, Non-Veg, and Other food categories
* Provide interactive analysis using toggle buttons

---

##  Features & Highlights

### 1️ Business Problem

Food delivery platforms operate across many cities and handle thousands of orders daily. Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to track revenue performance, delivery efficiency, and customer activity. Businesses need clear visual insights to understand trends and optimize their operations.

### 2️ Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this dashboard is to analyze the performance of a food delivery platform by visualizing revenue, customer activity, restaurant distribution, and delivery efficiency across multiple cities.

### 3️ Walkthrough of Key Visuals

* **KPI Cards** show important metrics such as Total Revenue, Average Order Value, Total Customers, Total Restaurants, Total Cities, and Delivery Areas.
* **City-wise Revenue Chart** compares revenue generated across different cities.
* **Delivery Time Analysis** shows average delivery time by city.
* **Food Category Distribution** visualizes the proportion of Veg, Non-Veg, and Other food orders.
* **Top Customers and Restaurants** identify the highest contributors to platform revenue.

### 4️ Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses:

* Identify high-performing cities generating the most revenue
* Monitor delivery efficiency across different locations
* Understand customer and restaurant distribution
* Track food category demand trends
* Support data-driven business decisions

### 5️ Insights

Some insights that can be derived from the dashboard include:

* Certain cities contribute more revenue compared to others.
* Delivery time varies across locations depending on operational efficiency.
* Some cities have a higher concentration of restaurants and customers.
* Non-Veg food orders tend to dominate in many regions.

## Interactive Feature (Amount vs Quantity)

One of the key features of this dashboard is the **interactive toggle buttons**.

Two buttons are available in the overview section:

**Amount Mode**
Shows revenue-based insights such as:

* Total Revenue
* Average Order Value
* Top Customers
* Top Restaurants

**Quantity Mode**
Shows operational insights such as:

* Total Customers
* Total Restaurants
* Total Cities
* Total Delivery Areas

This feature allows users to dynamically switch between financial metrics and operational metrics.
